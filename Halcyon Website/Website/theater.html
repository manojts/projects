Theater Events

1. Indian Ethnic Show
• 2 Teams Per College.
• A team should consist only of Boys or Girls only.
• Minimum 4 Participants and Maximum 8 Participants.
• Karaoke is allowed.
• Time limit : 6+1 minutes.
• Teams have to submit the photos/videos of their performance a week before the event.
• Decision of Judges is final & binding.
• Dress code of participants should be Strictly Indian & should not involve any kind of vulgarity.
2. Street Play (Open Event)
• Minimum 8 and maximum15 participants per team.
• Props are allowed.
• The play should be for a social cause.
• Music instruments are allowed.
• Time limit: 12+3 minutes.
3. Stand-Up Comedy
• Individual event.
• No vulgarity is allowed.
• Script should be submitted 10 min beforehand for verification.
• Time allowed is 3+2 min.
4. Skit (Open Event)
• Minimum of 6 and maximum 15 people including narrator and accompanist (if any) allowed.
• Duration of skit 10+2 minutes. A warning bell will be sounded after minimum time and a long bell after 12 minutes.
• Marks will be deducted for exceeding the time limit.
• Negative marks for vulgarity (usage of obscenities, obscene action and depictions).
• The skit performance should not be part of any movie or television series.
• Extra marks for innovative approach and humour (usage of props and costumes are allowed).
• Innovative themes and moral gists attract better points.
• The decision of the JUDGES is final and binding on participants.
5. 60 Seconds to Fame
• Open Event.
• Maximum of 2 participants per team.
• Judgment is based on creativity, innovation and uniqueness.
• The act can be dance, singing, \mimicry, magic, comical etc. as long as it is not obscene.
• Props and costumes are allowed.
• Time limit given is 60 seconds.
• Decision of the JUDGES is the final.
6. Mime
• Open Event.
• 6 participants per team.
• 2 accompanists are allowed.
• Background music is allowed.
• Time limit 4+2 min.
