Music Events

1. Light Indian Vocal Solo
• 3 Participants per college.
• Karaoke or 2 accompanists are allowed.
• Time limit 4+1 min.
• Violation of any rule will lead to disqualification.
2. Duet Singing
• 2 members per team.
• 3 teams per college are allowed.
• No gender restriction on team constitution.
• Karaoke or 2 accompanists are allowed.
• Duration of performance 4+1 min.
• Judgement will be based solely on vocals.
• Violation of any rule will lead to disqualification.
3. Instrumental Solo (Open Event)
• Individual(Solo) event.
• No recorded music is allowed.
• Shruti boxes, Nagma boxes and Taalmala allowed.
• Time limit: 5+1 minutes.
• Violation of any rule will lead to disqualification.
4. Classical Vocal Solo
• One classical piece to be chosen.
• Participant has to mention raga and taala.
• No recorded music is allowed.
• Shruti boxes allowed.
• Time limit: 8+1 minutes.
• Violation of any rule will lead to disqualification.
5. Western Vocal Solo
• 3 Participants per college.
• Karaoke or 2 accompanists are allowed.
• Time limit 4+1minutes.
• Violation of any rule will lead to disqualification.
6. Battle of Bands
• Maximum of Three Bands per College.
• Maximum of 7 participants per band.
• Cross college teams are not allowed.
• Songs performed by the band can be in any language.
• Time given is 20 minutes. That includes the time taken for sound check, performance and set up.
• Bands have to bring their own equipment (apart from the ones mentioned below).
• Drumset with double bass pedal.
• Adequate microphones.
• You can bring your own cymbals and chokes.
• Special effects, karaoke and programmed music is not allowed .
• Judges decision is final and binding.
• Violation of any rule will lead to disqualification.
7. Solo Beat boxing (Open Event)
• It is a 2 stage competition, elimination and finals.
• It is an open event.
• In elimination each participant has to show vocal percussion skills infront of the judges.
• In finals qualified participants must battle face to face on stage.
• In final battle two microphones will be provided with same consistent sound level.
• No instruments are allowed.
• Judges decision will be based on originality and techniques.
• Time limit in elimination round is 3 minutes and final round will be decided on spot.
• Halcyon committee reserves the right to make any last minute changes in the rules.
• Judges decision is final and binding.
