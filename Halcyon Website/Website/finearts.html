Fine Arts Events

1. Face painting (Open Event)
• Topic will be given on spot.
• Paints and brushes will be provided (maximum 6 colours).
• Provisions- Waste cloth/paper, Water, Plastic Cups.
• Time limit: 1 hour
2. Clay modeling (Open Event)
• Individual Event.
• No colours allowed.
• Topic will be given on the spot.
• Clay and water will be provided.
• No other materials must be used.
• Time limit: 1 hour 15 minutes.
3. Collage (Open Event)
• 2 participants per team.
• Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes.
• The topic will be given on the spot.
• Only chart paper and glue will be provided per team.
• Scissors and burning of papers prohibited.
• No other item will be provided.
• Participants will not be allowed to leave the hall during the event.
4. Installation (Open Event)
• 3 members per team.
• Theme will be provided 2 hrs prior to the event.
• No accessories will be provided.
• Damage to college property will attract heavy fine and disqualification.
• Only waste materials to be used.
• Use of transportation to find material is not allowed.
• Art must be explained.
• Duration of the event: 1 hr.
5. 70MM
• 3 Participants per team.
• Only cam video and flash animations are allowed.
• Compiled video/animations should be provided in CDs (.avi, .MPEG, .flv), USB portable media storage device.
• Downloaded videos are not allowed.
• Duration of the videos should be maximum 3 mins.
• The topic will be announced on the website 48 hours prior to the event.
6. Creative Photography
• Individual event.
• Only cam pictures should be produced in CDs (.jpg,.jpeg),USB portable media storage device.
• Downloaded photographs are not allowed.
• The topic will be announced on the website 48 hours prior to the event.
• The participants can produce only one photograph at the time of submission.
• Editing of photo is allowed.
7. Pencil Sketch (Open Event)
• Individual event.
• Duration: 45 minutes.
• The topic will be given on the spot.
• Only chart paper and stationeries will be provided.
• Scissors and burning of papers prohibited.
• No other item will be provided.
• Participants will not be allowed to leave the hall during the event.
